Mindspace

Each page is a different “room” built in BabylonJS. The site flows like a small game and each scene shows a different required module element.

Scenes overview

  • Scene 1 – Shapes: Basic shapes, light, shadows, rotation.
  • Scene 2 – Environment: Walkable space around the Leartes castle.
  • Scene 3 – Player: Player mesh, movement, simple physics.
  • Scene 4 – GUI: On-screen UI with audio controls.
  • Scene 5 – Switching: Swap between two different “mind rooms”.

Assets

All textures, models and sounds go into the assets/ folder. For now they’re placeholders until the final model/audio files are added.
