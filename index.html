Mindspace

Mindspace is a BabylonJS portfolio website containing five interactive 3D scenes. Each scene demonstrates a different area of JavaScript game development, from basic shapes and lighting through to player movement, GUI/audio systems, and scene switching.

The final scene is being developed as a small realm-travel demo inspired by The Withering of the World Tree, moving between Midgard, Yggdrasil, Asgard’s remnants, and another mythological realm.

Scenes overview

Scene 1 – Shapes

Demonstrates basic BabylonJS shapes, lighting, textures, shadows, motion, click interactions, and sound effects.

Scene 5 – Realm Switching

Assets and tools

The project uses BabylonJS, JavaScript, HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, imported models, generated geometry, textures, GUI elements, and audio assets. Sourced assets are stored in the assets/ folder and will be credited in the documentation.
